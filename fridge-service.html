Cleaning & Optimization

Regularly clean your fridge to prevent odors and bacteria, disposing of expired items and wiping surfaces with a mild detergent solution. Organize contents efficiently, placing frequently used items at eye level and grouping similar items together to optimize space. Adjust temperature settings for food freshness and energy savings, ensuring a well-maintained appliance.

  • Consistently clean shelves, drawers, and seals to prevent odors.
  • Arrange items strategically to maximize space and accessibility
  • Maintain optimal temperature settings for food freshness and energy efficiency.
