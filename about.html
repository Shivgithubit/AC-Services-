Why my air conditioner is not working properly

Possible issues: low refrigerant, dirty filter, thermostat problems, or compressor malfunction.

How do i simply reset my air conditioning unit

Turn off power, wait 5 minutes, then turn it back on. If problem persists, consult manual or technician.

Can i fix my ac problem in my home anyway

Yes, for basic issues like filter cleaning. Complex problems may need professional assistance for resolution.
